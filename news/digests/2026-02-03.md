## 2. HRW "The Gig Trap" Report — Algorithmic Wage Theft Documentation

**Source:** Human Rights Watch  
**Date:** May 12, 2025 (report); ongoing relevance  
**URL:** https://www.hrw.org/report/2025/05/12/the-gig-trap/

**Summary:** 155-page analysis examining **seven major US platforms**: Amazon Flex, DoorDash, Favor, Instacart, Lyft, Shipt, and Uber. Key findings:
- Algorithmic systems systematically extract labor while evading legal obligations
- Only Amazon Flex offers flat hourly wage; others use dynamic algorithms calculating pay post-completion
- "The illusion of autonomy masks algorithmic control and financial vulnerability"

**Relevance:** Primary source for algorithmic management critique. Evidence of systematic wage theft across platform types.

**Action items:**

- [ ] Extract case studies: Amazon Flex, DoorDash, Favor

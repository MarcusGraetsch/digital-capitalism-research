# Newsletter Research Database

Umfassende Liste von Newslettern für Digital Capitalism / Platform Studies / Future of Work Forschung

## 1. AKADEMISCH & THEORETISCH

### Platform Capitalism & Critical Theory
- **Logic Magazine** (logicmag.io)
  - Deep dives into tech, labor, power
  - Contributors: Wendy Liu, Ben Tarnoff, Moira Weigel
  - Essential for platform studies

- **Real Life Magazine** (reallifemag.com)
  - Culture & technology criticism
  - Often Marxist/sociological perspective

- **New Inquiry** (thenewinquiry.com)
  - Critical theory meets tech culture
  - Irregular but high quality

- **The Maintainers** (themaintainers.org)
  - Infrastructure studies, maintenance labor
  - Scholars: Lee Vinsel, Andrew Russell

- **LPE Blog** (lpeproject.org)
  - Law & Political Economy
  - Platform regulation, antitrust, labor law

### Science & Technology Studies (STS)
- **Science for the People** (magazine.scienceforthepeople.org)
  - STS from left perspective
  - Tech labor, military-industrial complex

- **Catalyst Journal** (catalyst-journal.com)
  - New left science journal
  - Irregular newsletter with essay digests

## 2. INVESTIGATIVER JOURNALISMUS

### Big Tech & Labor
- **Platformer** (platformer.news) - Casey Newton
  - Meta, Uber, Amazon insider coverage
  - Essential for big tech labor issues
  - Premium worth it

- **Big Technology** (bigtechnology.substack.com) - Alex Kantrowitz
  - Big tech accountability
  - Often covers worker organizing

- **The Information** (theinformation.com)
  - Hard tech business journalism
  - Uber, Amazon, gig economy deep dives

- **Rest of World** (restofworld.org)
  - Tech outside US/EU bubble
  - Global gig work, platform colonialism
  - Newsletter + longform

- **Wired** (wired.com/newsletter)
  - Sign up for "The Labor Issue" alerts

### Surveillance & Privacy
- **Surveillance Technology Oversight Project** (stopspying.org)
  - S.T.O.P. newsletter
  - Worker surveillance focus

- **The Markup** (themarkup.org)
  - Data journalism on tech harm
  - Algorithmic accountability
  - Newsletter: "Hello World"

- **Citizen Lab** (citizenlab.ca)
  - University of Toronto
  - Spyware, surveillance tech
  - Monthly digest
